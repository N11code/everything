Our Story

Everything Cold Enterprise, a Ghanaian based Enterprise duly registered under the registration of business names Act, 1962(no.151) with its principal office located at Weija on the premises of DVLA, Accra. Founded in 2021 and 100% owned by Ghanaian. We also have a branch located in UK. Our Company is a major contractor for sales, repairs and maintenance services of central air, split air, car air, cold rooms and solar panels. Our core values are:

  • Integrity
  • Partnership
  • Accountability
  • Innovation
  • loyalty
  • Safety

We combine the rich experience of our partners, both domestic and foreign, with our rich experience and knowledge of the Ghanaian terrain, as a prominent industry player, to ensure that we provide our clients with the best services available.
